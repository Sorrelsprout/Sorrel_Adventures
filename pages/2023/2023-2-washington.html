We stayed at a questionable AirBnB in Tacoma. After further investigation on the app, the host seemed very rude to anyone who left less than 5 stars for reviewing the place we were staying at. There was a microwave and sink, but no stovetop nor laundry (for people staying over a week), which was also something he blasted people about (for asking to do laundry when it was listed as a service on the AirBnB listing). Bad news but it was what it was, so we stayed. At least it wasn't a straight up shack like last December's.
