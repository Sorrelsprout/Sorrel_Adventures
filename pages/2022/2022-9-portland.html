Breakfast at hotel, In J Coffee at PS with dragon mural, Lady wanted directions to OSHU, Marquam nature park. Kept walking Rose garden. I thought the San Jose Rose Garden was expansive, but the Portland one was something else! So many different flower varieties, rows upon rows of flowers. Almost to Hoyt Arboretum but it was too dark and I was tired and really wanted to head back.
